Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information this Discord bot ("the Bot"), operated by Girik Chaos, collects, how it's used, and your choices regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

To provide its features, the Bot stores the following data:

  • Discord User IDs — used to track leveling progress, warnings, and game win streaks
  • Server (Guild) IDs — used to remember which roles belong to that server's leveling system
  • Experience points (XP) and level data — earned through chatting or playing mini-games
  • Moderation records — warnings issued by server staff, including the reason and moderator

The Bot does not permanently store the content of your messages. Message text is only read briefly and in real time to check for chat activity (for XP) or command usage, and is not saved afterward.

2. How We Use This Information

Collected data is used solely to operate the Bot's features — leveling, moderation logs, and game mechanics — within the Discord servers where the Bot is added. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and only for as long as necessary to provide the Bot's functionality. If the Bot is removed from a server, that server's associated data may be deleted at our discretion.

4. Your Rights

You can request that your data (such as XP, level, or warning history) be reset or deleted by contacting us using the information below. Server administrators may also request removal of their server's data.

5. Children's Privacy

The Bot is intended for use in accordance with Discord's own Terms of Service, which require users to meet Discord's minimum age requirement. We do not knowingly collect additional personal information beyond what Discord itself provides through normal bot operation.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes means you accept the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: Discord — girik_chaos
